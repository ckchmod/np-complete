THE LOCK

Open the lock — reverse the red arrow.

  • Tap an arrow to flip its direction.
  • Every node must always have at least 2 arrows pointing in — a thin arrow counts 1, a thick arrow counts 2.
  • You can only flip an arrow if every node stays satisfied.
  • A faint outline marks where the red arrow must end up.

A few quick puzzles teach you the rest, then pick as many locks as you can.
