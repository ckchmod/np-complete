RUSH

Pick as many locks as you can.

  • Every lock still opens the same way — reverse the red arrow.
  • Each lock gives you a move budget. Open it before you run out of moves.
  • You have three lives (✕ ✕ ✕). Running out of moves spends one.
  • Stuck? Skip the lock — it also costs a life, but you live to pick the next.

Three lives gone ends the run. How far can you get?

BATTLE

Win your target before the rival wins theirs.

  • Players take turns. White moves first.
  • Flip your own edges, or neutral edges when the current rules allow them.
  • Each edge has limited charges; spent edges cannot move.
  • Reverse your target edge to win.
  • No legal move on your turn means you lose.
  • In vs AI, Black thinks for a beat and moves automatically.

Hot-seat: pass the phone after each legal flip. Vs AI: you play White; Black thinks briefly, then moves.
